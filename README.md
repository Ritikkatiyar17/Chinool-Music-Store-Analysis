🎵 Chinook Music Store Analysis (SQL + Excel)

📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the Chinook Music Store database using SQL and Excel to uncover actionable business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product popularity, and revenue generation.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that could support data-driven business decisions, improve customer retention, and increase revenue.

🎯 Business Problem
The music store wanted to better understand:

Who are the most valuable customers?
Which customers are long-term loyal customers?
Which customers are at risk of churn or have already churned?
What genres and artists drive the most revenue?
Which countries and regions generate the highest sales?
How can customer retention and revenue growth be improved?

🔍 Key Insights

👥 Customer Insights
A small percentage of customers contributed a significant share of total revenue.
Several customers demonstrated strong long-term purchasing behavior, indicating high loyalty.
A group of customers showed no recent purchase activity and were classified as potential churn customers.
Repeat customers generated substantially higher revenue compared to one-time buyers.

🎵 Product Insights
Certain music genres consistently outperformed others in sales volume and revenue.
A small number of artists contributed disproportionately to total sales.
Popular genres varied across different geographic regions.

🌍 Geographic Insights
A few countries generated the majority of store revenue.
Some regions showed strong growth potential but lower customer penetration.
Sales concentration highlighted opportunities for localized marketing campaigns.

💡 Business Recommendations
Customer Retention
Launch loyalty and rewards programs for high-value customers.
Create personalized offers for long-term customers.
Implement re-engagement campaigns targeting churned customers.
Use customer segmentation for targeted marketing.
Revenue Growth
Promote top-performing genres and artists through featured campaigns.
Increase investment in high-revenue regions.
Develop region-specific promotions based on purchasing behavior.
Encourage repeat purchases through personalized recommendations.
Customer Lifecycle Strategy
Monitor customer activity to identify churn risks early.
Track customer lifetime value (CLV) to prioritize retention efforts.
Establish retention KPIs and customer health metrics.
